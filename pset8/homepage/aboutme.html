Throughout my life I have taken initiative and consideration in how I can best apply myself while living the life I want too. Less than a year ago I decided that my pursuit of success with entrepreneurship was not reliable enough. After talking to experts in different careers I decided the path of software developer took advantage of my tenacity as well as my interests.

Since a young age I have been very interested in computers and always had a logical approach to fixing things. I became more comfortable during high school with Linux and Python and even got a Raspberry Pi for my birthday! After resolving to learn programing I started with Youtube walkthroughs moving onto CS50 to get even more competent with computer science.

I am planning to create a web app to digitize records of deceased people in the care of funeral homes. By using JavaScript and SQL I can make a quicker, easier to use and more reliable recording system to modernise funeral homes still relying on pen and paper for sensitive records.
